## Opus Magnum AI Scheming
# Introduction
The goal of this project is to build an AI model that controls processes required to make deliberate schemes (i.e., plans focusing on successfully fulfilling given goals). The goals the model can plan for are, as for their variety, somewhat limited. In short, just the kinds of goals that pertain to areas which we consider important are taken into account. We do not want to build an AI model that helps make decisions about whether to buy this or that cone of ice cream, for instance.

Another important point to stress is that our AI model should be firmly based on practical experience. That does not necessarily mean that we could not leave space to conceive rather theoretical plans, but the plans should finally be feasible in the real world.
